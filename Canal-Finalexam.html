Titan Academy

Complete Having enhanced senses cannot help one earn a living. A useless special ability--or at least, that's what Shia Sheridan believes. But when she is caught for a crime she did not commit, this useless ability saves her, leading her to enroll in Titan Academy, the very place she hates with a passion.

***

Titan Academy is an esteemed school where students train to improve their special abilities. However, for seventeen-year-old village girl Shia Sheridan, it's nothing more than a despicable place where wealth speaks the loudest. Fate plays a joke on her when her best friend, Lucas, becomes part of a crime committed within the academy, and owning that crime in his stead leads Shia to the headmaster and principal, who then offers her a proposal she dare not refuse--in exchange for her freedom, she must become one of the special group of students joining the annual survival game called Linus Cup. Now, not only is she fighting for a spot she did not want in the very academy she hated, but also for her dear life as well. Can Shia survive till the end?

Willford Boarding School

I am not your typical nerd girl that you can find in the books or movies. I spenda my life owning almost everything. Money, supportive and kind family, pretty face and a body to die for, talents and intelligence. Why am i pretending to be a looser? -- My best friend forced me to make a bet to relieve our boredom. I accepted it because i know it will be fun. I was so close! 5 months before the graduation and I will win the bet. Not until that very unfateful day.

I always wished to experience something exciting aside from bungee jumping, mountain climbing, hunting in the wild and even participating street fights. Unfortunately, i don't feel any excitement doing those dangerous stunts. BUT not in my dreams considering fighting zombies is fun.. Yeah! You're reading it right. Zombie outbreak is happening in the city and the safest place we have to survive is our school. The Willford Boarding School.
